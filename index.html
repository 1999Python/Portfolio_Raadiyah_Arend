About Me

Hello there! I'm Raadiyah, a dedicated Full-Stack Developer driven by three essential qualities I believe are the cornerstone of successful coding: Problem Solving, Perseverance, and Patience. My journey into the world of web development began with an intensive 8-month BootCamp covering both front and back-end development, complemented by CS50's Web programming with Python and JavaScript.

My aspiration in this field is to continue crafting innovative web solutions, collaborating with brilliant minds, and contributing to projects that leave a positive mark. I thrive on new challenges and opportunities that allow me to push the boundaries of what's possible.

Outside of coding, I'm an avid explorer of the great outdoors, a culinary enthusiast always eager to experiment with new recipes, and a passionate chess player. I firmly believe that a well-rounded life beyond work is a wellspring of creativity and inspiration.
